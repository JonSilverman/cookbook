TACOS GALORE

Ingredients
- tortillas, soft or hard
- taco spices
- diced chile peppers
- 1 pound of hamburger
- shredded lettuce
- shredded cheese
- salsa

Directions
1. brown the hamburger in large frying pan on medium heat; drain the fat and return the pan and hamburger to the stove
2. add the taco seasoning, diced chiles and 1/2 cup salsa to the hamburger; let simmer for 10 minutes
3. serve taco mix in tortillas with lettuce, cheese and salsa
